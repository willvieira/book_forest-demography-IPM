9  Random effects

In this section, I discuss the plot random effects affecting growth, survival, and recruitment intercepts. Random effects were used to control for unknown variances grouped within the plot level. For each vital rate and species, the variance among plots was defined by the parameter \(\sigma_{plot}\) where random effects were generated with \(N(0, \sigma_{plot})\).
